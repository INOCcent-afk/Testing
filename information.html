32°C PHILIPPINES is a visual guide that explores the ramifications of our increasingly warmer planet through five locations around the philippines whose natural and cultural heritage is threatened by climated change.

Each site is a window into our changing world-- and insight into what the twenty first century may have in store for many more of us, in a thousands locations around the world,as climate change accelerates.
